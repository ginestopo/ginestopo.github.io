About me.

My name is Ginés. I studied science at Santa Ana's School in Seville, Spain. After finishing with an average of 8.27/10, I decided to study what I loved the most: Telecommunications Engineering at University of Seville, degree that is giving me the knowledge to develop new tecnologies. Since 2019 I started working as an intern at the Electronics Department at ETSI (University of Seville), helping in investigations such as Lab-On Chips applied on Retinosis Pigmentosa or developing new COVID PCR systems. I love experimenting in any field of tecnology and computing: 3D modeling and rendering, Music Production, Engineering Simulations, programming Languages and much more, using multiples softwares which I always try to be Opensource (go Opensource!) I feel confortable in an international environment due to I have travelled to England and US, to improve my English skills and Poland (Warsaw) to practise piano due I'm a big fan of Chopin and his music. Although I was in a conservatory for 4 years, I continue learning and practising piano by my self.

Fields

Some of the fields I'm good at.

Programming

I started programming in C as a self taught before entering University. Later at University I learnt some Java, Html, JavaScript, CSS, SQL... I also started experimenting with Python, my favourite language. Feel free to have a look at some of my codes at: My github repositories!

3D Modeling and rendering

I always thought that the best way of showing an idea is to create it. 3D models help to achieve that. That's why I learnt the Opensource software Blender. As a plus, if you know how to model you know to to 3D print ;)

Solving Engineering Problems

I love to see problems as a challenge. My degree gave me enough knowledge to find a solution to a problem in a wide variety of fields, like System Control, Electronics (Analog and Digital), Networks Protocols, Physics and Mathematics, Signal Processing, communications theory and much more...

Music

I started playing violin at the age of 7 but switched to piano at the age of 8. Since then, I studied piano for 4 years. Nowadays I continue studying piano by my self. My passion for Chopin and classical music led me to visit Warsaw (Poland) in 2019. I also felt curious at an early age about producing music and composing: You can hear some of my works down below :)

Videogames Development

I've always been amazed of the power of videogames to tell a story and make you feel totally in another dimension. Being aware that I couldn't make alone the next GTA; I started to learn how to develop videogames, first by coding (lot of coding!), and then, when I knew how a game works,by chosing an engine. In this case Godot Engine, which I currently use.

Others

While I'm not studying, I try to learn about something new, like video/photo editing, starting a new electronic project, learning about AI and Deeplearning...
