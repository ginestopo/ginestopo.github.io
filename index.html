About me.

My name is Ginés. I studied science at Santa Ana's School in Seville, Spain. After finishing with an average of 8.27/10, I decided to study what I loved the most: Telecommunications Engineering at University of Seville, degree that is giving me the knowledge to develop new tecnologies. Since 2019 I started working as an intern at the Electronics Department at ETSI (University of Seville), helping in investigations such as Lab-On Chips applied on Retinosis Pigmentosa or developing new COVID PCR systems. In 2021 I received an scholarship to study engineering in Poznan University of Technology for one year. In September of the same year I was accepted to be part of PUT Rocket Lab Team, where I'm in charge of developing a GPS Tracker system for the next generation Rocket HEXA2. I love experimenting in any field of tecnology and computing: 3D modeling and rendering, Music Production, Engineering Simulations, programming Languages and much more, using multiples softwares which I always try to be Opensource (go Opensource!) I feel confortable in an international environment due to I have travelled to England and US, to improve my English skills and Poland (Warsaw) to practise piano due I'm a big fan of Chopin and his music. Although I was in a conservatory for 4 years, I continue learning and practising piano by my self.

For more information, titles, recognisions or hire, please contact me at ginestopo@gmail.com
